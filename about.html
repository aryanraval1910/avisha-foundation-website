Our Story

A foundation built on showing up

Avisha Foundation is a registered charitable trust (Reg. No. E/9169/Vadodara) based in Sayajipura, Vadodara. It was founded on a simple idea: that the biggest gaps in a struggling family's life are rarely abstract — they're a missed meal, an untreated illness, a daughter with no way to earn, a child who can't afford to stay in school.

So instead of spreading thin across many causes, the Foundation works deliberately across four connected pillars — Mid-Day Meal, Women Empowerment, Medical Support and Education Support — and stays close to the communities it serves in and around Vadodara.

  • Registered charitable trust — E/9169/Vadodara
  • Four active pillars: Meal, Women, Medical, Education
  • Community-first, on-ground approach in Vadodara

Founder

Mr. Shivabhai Vankar

Mr. Shivabhai Vankar established Avisha Foundation on the belief that meaningful change begins at the community level. With deep roots in Vadodara, he set in motion the Foundation's four-pillar mission — ensuring that no child goes hungry, no woman lacks opportunity, no family is denied healthcare, and no student is forced to abandon their education for want of resources.

Under his founding vision, the Foundation has grown into a trusted presence in and around Sayajipura, working quietly and consistently where the need is greatest.
