About Us

Avisha Foundation

Avisha Foundation is a registered charitable trust (Reg. No. E/9169/Vadodara) based in Sayajipura, Vadodara, working quietly and consistently across four areas that touch a family's daily life most directly: food, women's independence, healthcare and education.

Founded by Mr. Shivabhai Vankar, the Foundation partners with local communities rather than working at a distance from them — showing up where the need is, and staying long enough to make a difference.

  • Registered charitable trust — E/9169/Vadodara
  • Four active pillars: Meal, Women, Medical, Education
  • Community-first, on-ground approach in Vadodara

What We Do

Four pillars, one purpose

Every programme we run sits under one of these four areas — chosen because together, they cover what a struggling family needs most.

01

Mid-Day Meal

Daily nourishing meals for children so hunger never comes in the way of learning.

02

Women Empowerment

Skill training and livelihood support that helps women stand on their own.

03

Medical Support

Health camps and treatment assistance for families who can't afford to wait.

04

Education Support

Fees, books and mentoring so promising students don't have to drop out.
