Our Work

Home/Our Work

Everything Avisha Foundation does sits under one of four pillars. Together they cover what a struggling family needs most: food on the table, a path to independence, access to basic healthcare, and a real chance at education.

01

Mid-Day Meal

Hunger is one of the simplest reasons a child stops going to school. Our Mid-Day Meal programme prepares and serves nourishing daily meals, so that classrooms stay full and children have the energy to actually learn.

  • Daily hot, nourishing meals
  • Focused on school-going children
  • Run in partnership with local communities
02

Women Empowerment

We work with women who want a real path to independence — through skill training, livelihood support and steady mentorship — so that the decisions about their lives stay in their own hands.

  • Vocational and skill-based training
  • Livelihood and income support
  • Ongoing mentorship
03

Medical Support

Basic healthcare shouldn't depend on how much a family can afford. We organise medical camps and help arrange treatment assistance for people who would otherwise go without care.

  • Community medical camps
  • Treatment assistance for families in need
  • Basic health awareness sessions
04

Education Support

A promising student shouldn't have to drop out over fees or books. We support school and tuition fees, learning materials, and mentoring, so students of limited means can stay on the path of learning.

  • Fee and tuition assistance
  • Books and learning materials
  • Ongoing mentoring for students

Your support keeps all four pillars running.

Every contribution goes directly into meals served, women trained, patients treated and students supported.
